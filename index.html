At Iris Fashions, we:

embrace ethical sourcing

We carefully source all our materials from local artisans, ensuring that each piece of fabric is crafted with love and respect for traditional techniques, supporting sustainable practices and empowering communities.

commit to mindful production

Our garments are thoughtfully made in Coimbatore, where we focus on small-batch production to minimize waste, reduce our environmental footprint, and celebrate craftsmanship over mass manufacturing.

champion comfort and quality

Each piece is designed with your comfort in mind, using 100% fine cotton for breathability, durability, and a luxurious feel, ensuring that every wear is a step towards long-lasting satisfaction.
